Baha 'Bar

Baha Ba’r, meaning “Flood Bar”, is a charming restaurant and bar located in the heart of Maite village, just 1 km south of San Juan and 8 km from Siquijor port. Designed to blend traditional island wood architecture with Spanish influences and stone elements, it offers a unique dining experience that reflects the island’s rich heritage.

Wakanda Restaurant & Bar

Wakanda Restaurant & Bar in Barangay Tambisan, Siquijor, offers an exciting blend of Spanish cuisine, live music, and a vibrant nightlife. With its warm ambiance, DJ performances, and late-night bar service, it’s a top spot for both dining and entertainment on the island.

Luca Loco

Luca Loko, located in San Juan, Siquijor, is a fusion restaurant that artfully combines local Filipino ingredients with international culinary styles, offering diverse vegetarian and vegan options. The restaurant is known for its commitment to using fresh, high-quality ingredients in every dish. With its cozy and stylish atmosphere, Luca Loko provides a memorable dining experience that captures the island's unique charm.

Wonderland Restaurant

Wonderland Siquijor is a fully vegan restaurant in San Juan that offers a fusion menu with options like smoothie bowls, tofu scramble, curry, and soba noodles. They are known for making everything in-house, from coconut milk and tofu to tahini and bread1. The restaurant is part of a picturesque hostel with a relaxed atmosphere and friendly staff.

Hidden Valley Bar & Resto

Hidden Valley Bar & Resto, nestled in the scenic landscapes of Lazi, Siquijor, offers an authentic Filipino dining experience with fresh, local ingredients and stunning natural views. This culinary haven is known for its diverse menu of traditional Filipino dishes, refreshing beverages, warm hospitality, and a serene ambiance, making it an ideal spot to unwind after exploring the island.
