Coco Grove Beach Resort

Located in San Juan near the Tubod Marine Sanctuary, Coco Grove Beach Resort offers a luxurious and comfortable stay for families and travelers seeking a pampered vacation. The resort features comfortable rooms with air conditioning, private bathrooms, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Guests can enjoy multiple pools and restaurants serving both local and international cuisine. With its beautiful sunset views from the white sandy beach, it's an ideal spot to relax and unwind.

Apo Diver Beach Resort

Situated in San Juan, just a few steps from Solangon Beach, Apo Diver Beach Resort provides comfortable air-conditioned rooms with furnished balconies. This beachfront resort boasts an outdoor swimming pool, a garden, barbecue facilities, free Wi-Fi, and complimentary private parking. Guests appreciate the convenient location, friendly staff, and the overall excellent amenities offered. It's a great choice for those looking to enjoy the beach and explore the nearby Siquijor Town.

Kahoy Cottages

Located in Siquijor, Kahoy Cottages offers a charming and relaxing stay with a garden and a shared lounge. Guests can enjoy garden views and take advantage of the free Wi-Fi throughout the property. The cottages provide a cozy atmosphere, making it a popular choice for couples and travelers seeking a tranquil escape. The location provides easy access to the island's attractions while offering a peaceful retreat.

Salamangka Resort

Situated in San Juan, a few steps from Salamangka Beach, this resort offers accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden, and a terrace. The resort is known for its stunning pool, outstanding facilities, spacious rooms, and delicious food. Guests can enjoy a luxurious stay with exceptional service and a prime beachfront location. It is considered one of the best luxury hotels in Siquijor.

Nakabalo Guesthouse & Restaurant

Located a 13-minute walk from Maite Beach in Siquijor, Nakabalo Guesthouse & Restaurant offers comfortable accommodations with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden, and a terrace. Guests can enjoy free WiFi, toiletries, and lush garden views from their rooms2. The on-site restaurant specializes in Spanish and Italian cuisine, providing a delightful dining experience. It’s a popular choice, highly rated by couples, offering a blend of comfort and convenience1.
