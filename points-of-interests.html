Paliton Beach

Situated near San Juan, Paliton Beach is known as one of the most beautiful beaches on Siquijor, boasting white sand, clear waters, and swaying palm trees. This serene location offers a tranquil escape, perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying breathtaking sunsets. Despite its popularity, Paliton Beach maintains a relaxed atmosphere, making it an ideal spot to unwind and appreciate the island's natural beauty. Starfish can also be spotted in theclear waters, adding to the beach's allure.

San Isidro Labrador Church (Lazi Church)

Found in the municipality of Lazi, the San Isidro Labrador Church, also known as Lazi Church, is a remarkable historical and cultural landmark. Constructed in 1884, it stands as one of Asia's oldest and largest stone churches, showcasing stunning architecture and a rich religious heritage. Visitors can explore the nearby Convent Museum to discover religious artifacts and historical relics, providing insights into the island's past. Attending religious services or the annual Feast of San Isidro Labrador in May offers a deeper cultural experience.

Lugnason Falls

Nestled in the municipality of San Juan, Lugnason Falls is a hidden gem renowned for its natural beauty and peaceful ambiance. A short hike through the jungle leads to this serene waterfall, where cool, clear waters cascade into a natural pool. Swimming in the invigorating waters and exploring the surrounding forest trails offer a refreshing and immersive experience in nature. The falls provide a tranquil escape from the more crowded tourist spots.

Old Enchanted Balete Tree

This ancient Balete tree, estimated to be around 400 years old, is a popular and quick stop for tourists. Located on the way to other Siquijor attractions, the tree is believed to be enchanted, adding to its mystique. A freshwater pool at the base of the tree allows visitors to dip their feet and experience the unique ambiance of this natural wonder. While some tours are offered, a visit to the Balete Tree is often a brief but memorable part of exploring Siquijor.

Cambugahay Falls

Located in the town of Lazi, Cambugahay Falls is a stunning three-tiered waterfall with natural pools perfect for swimming and enjoying rope swings. The cascading waters and lush surroundings create a picturesque scene, making it a favorite spot for both locals and tourists seeking a refreshing escape. Its accessible location and playful atmosphere make it a must-visit destination on the island1. You can easily spend a few hours here enjoying the cool waters and natural beauty.
